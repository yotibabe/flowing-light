The "Luminescent Parchment" Concept

This design system treats the screen as a gallery space rather than a software interface. It prioritizes the "weight" of intellectual content through high-end editorial layouts.

  • No-Line Rule: Avoid 1px solid borders for structural sectioning. Use background shifts (surface to surface_container) or generous whitespace.
  • Ghost Border Fallback: If a separator is required, use `outline-variant` at 30% opacity.
  • Ambient Shadows: Hierarchy is established through Tonal Layering and diffused light (`shadow-2xl` with `on-surface/5` opacity) rather than harsh physical shadows.
  • Ethereal Gradients: Soft, multi-stop radial gradients to create depth in hero backgrounds.

Editorial Cards (Vibrant & Borderless)

Image-filled cards use `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`, and must be completely borderless (`border-none`). No white edges. No dark overlays. The image is bright by default, text is strictly left-aligned, and hover states trigger elevation (`-translate-y-1`) and scale, not color reveals.

4. Layout Patterns (布局模式)

1. Standard Navigation (标准导航)

Touch-friendly links with `min-h-[44px] min-w-[44px]`. Hover states use a 1px expanding border from the left origin. Never use `overflow-x-auto` or `whitespace-nowrap` on mobile. Ensure `gap-2` to `gap-4` on mobile so items fit perfectly on a 320px screen.

1. 内容的骨架：建立坐标系

在动荡的环境中，个体的叙事往往比宏大的理论更具有穿透力。我们的每一场分享，都是在试图还原那些真实的挣扎与突破。

"保持轻盈，但在核心价值上扎根极深。"

这是我们在设计这个系统时，始终贯彻的原则。不追求表面的繁复，而是让光影与留白去说话。
